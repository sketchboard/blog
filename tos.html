  5. Sketchboard Subscription Plans

    7scales offers you the option of subscribing to a monthly Subscription Plan, the Service is billed in advance on a monthly basis. Business boards are not subject to limitations placed on Free Plan. Business Boards are not publicly available and need to be explicitely shared with others, means provided by Sketchboard Service.

    Sketchboard subscription plans support versioned boards. Board versions are saved to Amazon S3. Images related to versions are stored on S3. Files have Amazon S3 promized 99.999999999% durability. Board versions share the same quota that is given for account images, see image support.

    Sketchboard subscription plan supports uploading images. Images are stored on image library and on boards. Physically images are stored on Amazon S3 that have 99.999999999% durability.

    Each plan have common and different capabilities. Plans are defined on pricing page and are subject to change from time to time. Available storage space is defined in the plan that user has purchased. Space is shared between images and board versions.

    Legacy Business Plan (Subscription fee is based on number or users in the team). Each user in the business account increases available space with 100 MB. One user account has 100 MB, two users account has 200 MB for files and so on. Space is shared with board versions.

    NOTE! Due to expenses coming for storing images and versions. There will be cleanup for the images and board versions. After a business account has expired, you have 14 days to reactivate your account until images and versions are removed from the account forever without possibility to recover those. Images will be shown as empty on boards that are using removed images.

    You may purchase Sketcboard Subscription Plan through Sketchboard Service. 7scales uses Stripe to manage payments. Sketchboard does not keep credit card information on its service. Payments are seller initiated and are charged on monthly basis. Subscription fee is based on number of users in the account.
