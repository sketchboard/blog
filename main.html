All Paid Plans

All paid plans support images and version control. Images and board versions are stored on Amazon S3 that has 99.999999999% durability. Paid plans support password sharing up to 3 collaborators/board at the same time. Export boards to GitHub, Flowdock, Google Drive or download boards as SVG, PNG or PDF. Search diagrams using visual index or keyword match. Publish board PNG publicly and embed in your web page.

Free Plans

Anonymous

You can test Sketchboard.io without sign up. Anyone can edit boards that are not owned and take ownership of the board. The first who access anonymous public board with signed up account takes the board ownership.

Signed Up

All boards are publicly available as read only, but are editable only by team members. You can have up to 3 realtime collaborators at the same time. Export diagrams directly to your public GitHub projects. Team will be automatically transferred to Free plan either when trial or paid subscription is expired. Your paid boards will not become public on expiration.
